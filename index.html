A Puppeteer's Journey

A young woman finds herself stranded in an unfamiliar city, with no idea who she is and why she is there. Though she attempts to leave, there is no exit in sight, and no one in the city seems to have any conception of the life outside of it. She is given shelter by an elderly puppeteer, given the name of "Citrus". Citrus helps with her work as she works to master the art of puppetry and perhaps find out just who she is. Follow Citrus's journey in this text-based adventure game with over 20 possible endings.

Iris Academy

The Dreamlands was once a lifeless land, said to be impossible to survive for even the hardiest man or beast. Until two hundred years ago, when the incarnated spirit Theresa transformed the earth and built a vast school within which her apostles could live, learn, and play. Today, it is now one of the very few places on Lyfnaheim where those from any sentient race can rub figurative shoulders together as equals. With the beginning of a new school year comes another generation of youths...
